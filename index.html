The "One-Armed Scientist"

The name "oneAS" stands for One-Armed Scientist—a direct tribute to a legendary United States Senate hearing where Senator Edmund Muskie grew frustrated with scientists hedging their answers with "on the one hand... but on the other hand."

"I want to find a One-Armed Scientist—so my experts will finally be physically incapable of saying, 'On the other hand!'"

We guarantee our clients that we will never hide behind the safe neutrality of "on the other hand" data dumps. We provide a clear, single-handed verdict so you can stop analyzing and start acting.
