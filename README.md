## 📖 The Origin of "oneAS"

The name "oneAS" stands for **One-Armed Scientist**—a direct tribute to a legendary 1970s United States Senate hearing led by Senator Edmund Muskie.

> During an intense cross-examination regarding environmental pollutants, Muskie grew deeply frustrated with a panel of academic and data experts. Every time he asked a direct question about the data's meaning, the scientists would hedge their answers, stating: *"Well, Mr. Senator, on the one hand, the numbers point to X... but on the other hand, the variables could suggest Y."*
>
> Exasperated by the back-and-forth indecisiveness, Muskie famously cut them off and declared that he wanted to find a **"One-Armed Scientist"**—so his experts would finally be physically incapable of saying, *"On the other hand!"*

### Our Brand Promise
We stand against hedging. We guarantee our clients that we will never hide behind the safe neutrality of "on the other hand" data dumps. We are not just a data processing vendor; we are a bold, trusted advisory partner that possesses the technical courage to deliver a clear, single-handed verdict on complex analytics.
